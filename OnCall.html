Unable to make it to our general session but still need some extra help? Look no further than our On Call Tutoring Program!

On Call Tutoring is a private tutoring session that works around your schedule. You get to choose the time and day that accommodates you and we match you up for a personalized session with one of our highly skilled tutors.



  • How do I book an On Call Tutoring session?
    • On Call Tutoring is easy to set up! Just click on the link above and you will be taken to a professional booking website where you will be able to select your preferred time. Each session is one hour by default and starts at the time you selected. For example, if you choose 6:00 pm on Wednesday, Sept 30th, the tutoring session will be from 6:00 pm to 7:00 pm on Sept 30th. We will contact you within 2 business days to confirm the location of the session.
  • How would I pay for my booked session?
    • You can pay directly on our website as you book. Your payment is processed via Stripe, a payment system used by high-profile services such as Fitbit, Twitter, Reddit and The Guardian. If you would like to book in person, swing by one of our general tutoring sessions at the times listed on our home page and we would be happy to help you out.
  • How do I reschedule or cancel?
    • You can easily cancel up to 24 hours before the booked session. This is done by simply clicking on the "CANCEL this booking" link at the end of your confirmation email. If you need to reschedule, simply cancel the booked session and book a different one. You will refunded 100% upon cancelling your sessions.
