Students for Learning
High School Chapter

is a not for profit tutoring organization started in September of 2014. Coming up on 1 year as a complete group, SFLHSC continues to uphold our core value of continual improvement, on both the administrative side and on the tutoring side of the organization. We have recently introduced our new website, and seek to build on our relationships with High Schools throughout local Edmonton. We currently operate with an Executive Team of 7 members and are progressively advertising for and hiring new volunteer tutors every month. If you are looking for high quality, free tutoring, come by for a visit sometime (see our home page for dates and locations). No sign up or registration is required.
