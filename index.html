High School Chapter

is a group at the University of Alberta dedicated to providing free tutoring at the High School level. If you are a parent or student interested in our services, feel free to stop by our drop-in tutoring centre at the time and place shown below!

No registration is required for our tutoring centre!

Our 2015-2016 Tutor Team

Our next session is Saturday, May 14 from 1-3 p.m.

The tutoring center will be located in ECHA 1-490. See you there!

The Edmonton Clinic Health Academy (ECHA) is located on the University of Alberta campus, on the corner of 114th Street NW and 87th Avenue NW. This area is accessible easiest via the High Level Bridge (109th Street NW) or via Groat Road NW (Exit toward Hawrelak Park). There is parking located in the Education Car Park or in the Jubilee Car Park, and both of these parking areas are $5.00 after 4:30 PM. Please note there may be restrictions on parking at the Jubilee Car Park if there is a special event at the Northern Alberta Jublilee Auditorium. This area is also serviced by Edmonton Transit Service LRT, with the closest stop being Health Sciences/Jubilee Station. Also, buses on routes 4, 51, 57, 94, 106, 128, 130, and 313 make stops in the area of ECHA.

By clicking the Google logo in the bottom left corner of the above map, you can head over to Google Maps and plan out your trip to our location! The address of ECHA is 11405 87th Ave. NW Edmonton, AB.
